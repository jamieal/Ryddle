About Ryddle

Ryddle is a quizical inspired game that challenges you to guess the answer to a riddle within the following criteria:

  • You have 5 guesses
  • The answer may contain words like "a" or "an". You won't be penalised if you don't use it.
  • If you guess 5 times incorrectly, the riddle will be shown to you.
  • Only one Ryddle a day!
